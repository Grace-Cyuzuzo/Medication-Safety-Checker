# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

KPIs help quantify prescribing safety, patient risk, and medicine interaction frequency. These KPIs are used across dashboards to summarize performance.

---

## - High-Severity Alert Rate
**Formula:**  
`(Major Alerts / Total Alerts) Ã— 100`

**Purpose:**  
Shows how dangerous the interaction patterns are.

---

##  Prescription Interaction Ratio
**Formula:**  
`Prescriptions with Alerts / Total Prescriptions`

**Purpose:**  
Measures overall prescription safety.

---

##  Top Interacting Medicine Pair
**Definition:**  
The medicine pair with the highest number of alert occurrences.

**Purpose:**  
Helps identify medicines requiring review or special cautions.

---

##  Doctor Alert Rate
**Formula:**  
`Alerts Generated by Doctor / Total Prescriptions by Doctor`

**Purpose:**  
Evaluates prescribing safety per doctor.

---

##  Repeat High-Risk Patient Count
**Definition:**  
Number of patients with 2 or more Major alerts in 6 months.

**Purpose:**  
Identifies vulnerable patients needing closer monitoring.

---

##  Monthly Alert Trend
**Formula:**  
Count of alerts per month.

**Purpose:**  
Tracks safety improvements or deterioration over time.
