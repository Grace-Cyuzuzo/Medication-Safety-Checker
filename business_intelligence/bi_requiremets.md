# Bi_requirements

## 1. Purpose of BI in Medication Safety Checker
The BI component transforms raw alert and prescription data into actionable insights. It enables doctors, pharmacists, and hospital management to monitor safety, track risk trends, and improve clinical outcomes.
	
## Patients Safety Monitoring

- Which medicine commonly cause major interactions.
- Which patients repeatedly recieve risky combinations.

## Prescription Risky analysis

- What percentage of prescription that generate alerts.
- Which doctors generate the highest number of severe alerts?

## Medicine Risk Analytics

- What are the top interacting medicine pairs?

- Which categories of medicines cause the most interactions?

## Operational Insights

- How many interactions occur in a month?

- How often do doctors change prescriptions after interactions?
