Each dashboard includes its purpose, key visualizations, and the insights it provides to support decision-making.

### **Purpose**
Evaluate prescribing safety for each doctor and identify areas where training or support is needed.

### **Key Visualizations**
1. **Column Chart ‚Äì Alerts Per Doctor**
   - Shows number of alerts generated by each doctor.
   - Identifies doctors who prescribe risky combinations frequently.

2. **Pie Chart ‚Äì Severity Breakdown Per Doctor**
   - Shows percentage of Major, Moderate, and Minor alerts per doctor.
   - Flags doctors with high rates of severe interactions.

3. **Ranked Table ‚Äì Doctor Risk Score**
   - Combines total alerts and severity to rank doctors.
   - Helps determine who needs follow-up support or training.

### **Insights Provided**
- Which doctors need help with safer prescribing?
- Which doctors produce many high-severity alerts?
- Are training efforts reducing doctor risk levels?

## üßë‚Äçü¶Ω Dashboard 3: Patient Safety Dashboard

### **Purpose**
Identify high-risk patients who frequently experience dangerous interactions and require closer medical monitoring.

### **Key Visualizations**
1. **List ‚Äì High-Risk Patients**
   - Shows patients with repeated Major interactions.
   - Useful for targeted follow-ups and clinical interventions.

2. **Line Chart ‚Äì Patient Alerts Over Time**
   - Displays alert history for each patient.
   - Helps clinicians track patient safety trends.

3. **Risk Score Table**
   - Calculates risk score based on severity:
     - Major = 3 points
     - Moderate = 2 points
     - Minor = 1 point
   - Ranks patients by severity-weighted risk.

### **Insights Provided**
- Which patients need urgent review?
- Are specific patients repeatedly getting dangerous medications?
